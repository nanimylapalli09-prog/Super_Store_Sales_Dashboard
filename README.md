

 Retail Sales Dashboard | Microsoft Excel

 Project Overview

This project showcases an interactive Retail Sales Dashboard developed in Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance and generate actionable business insights. The dashboard transforms raw retail sales data into a clear and visually engaging report using Excel’s data analysis and visualization features.

It enables users to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, compare regional performance, and evaluate product category performance to support data-driven decision-making.



 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance.
* Identify the highest-performing product categories.
* Track monthly sales trends.
* Compare sales across different regions.
* Evaluate product-wise sales contribution.
* Present key business metrics in an interactive dashboard.

 Dashboard Features

 Key Business Insights

* Technology is the highest-performing product category, contributing the largest share of total sales.
* Monthly sales exhibit noticeable fluctuations, indicating possible seasonal demand and promotional impacts.
* The West region generates the highest sales, making it the strongest-performing market.
* The North region records the lowest sales, highlighting potential opportunities for market expansion.
* A small number of products contribute the majority of total revenue, while several products show comparatively low sales performance.
